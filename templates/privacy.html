Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter into the app.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the Service, such as pages visited and features used.

We do not collect payment information, government-issued identification, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used solely to provide and improve the Service. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Display your expense history and spending summaries within the app.
  • Authenticate your account and keep your data secure.
  • Diagnose bugs and improve the reliability of the Service.
  • Send account-related communications (e.g. password resets) when requested.

We will never sell your personal data or use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may delete your account at any time. Upon deletion, your personal information and expense records will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes. We may use trusted third-party services to help operate the Service (e.g. hosting providers), and these parties are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only as instructed.

We do not integrate with external financial institutions or payment processors, so no banking credentials are ever transmitted through Spendly.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie contains no personal information and is deleted when you log out or close your browser. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding your personal data — including requests to access or delete your data — please contact us through the app or at the email address associated with your account.
